🏆 Production Deployment Recommendation
DenseNet121 v1.0 is the recommended production model. It achieves the highest ROC-AUC (97.89%), Accuracy (94.23%), and Recall (97.01%) among all evaluated architectures. With only 7.70M parameters — far fewer than ResNet50's 24.77M — it provides superior clinical performance with significantly lower computational overhead. Its 97.01% recall is especially critical in a medical screening context, minimising dangerous false negatives (missed pneumonia cases). The model is currently flagged is_production = 1 in the database and actively deployed. EfficientNetB0 is recommended as a lightweight backup or edge-deployment alternative.
